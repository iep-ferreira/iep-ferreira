About Me

Iuri has BSc in Health Physics and MSc in Biometrics from São Paulo State University (IBB/UNESP), and PhD in Statistics from University of São Paulo (ESALQ/USP), Brazil. He has experience in design of experiments, regression models, and statistics applied to biological, agricultural, and environmental data. Currently, Iuri is a professor at the Federal University of São Carlos and coordinates the Center of Statistical Modelling on Evironmental Sciences (Ce-MECA), working in the interface between R programming, mathematical modelling, simulation, and statistics.
